About BPS:

Situated in the Kathua district of Jammu and Kashmir, Billawar Public school is a highly acclaimed educational body that came into existence in the year 1990. Managed by Private Unaided and affiliated by the state board, this co-ed school runs classes from 1st to 12th standard. The school contains well-furnished classrooms with good amenities and standardized infrastructure. The library is packed with about 1550 books and the computer lab consists of two functional computers. There is a total of 16 faculty members, all well versed in their respective streams.
